About Idea Manager

Idea Manager is a lightweight web application designed to help teams brainstorm, track, and manage their innovative ideas. From initial brainstorming to completion tracking, it provides a simple dashboard to keep everything in one place.

Features include:

  • Idea submission and tracking
  • Member management
  • Status and completion monitoring
  • Dark mode support
